Here is a showcase of my best models. I first learned how to make 3D models using Maya, and then I switched to Blender later on. Every model showcased here was created and textured by me. The textures were made using Adobe Substance Painter and Blender. Screenshots were taken in either their respective program or the project where they were used. Some models were created for projects, while others were created for fun. For each image, I will include a description as well as anything interesting that happened during the creation. Try hovering over the pictures, you may see an alternate picture.

Funky Mungos

This is a collage of various models for the game "Funky Mungos", a semester long project (Fall 2023) where we created a video game. I was the Technical Artist, creating all the models and animations.

Black Frost Has a Message

This is a simple render I made for the game "Shin Megami Tensei V: Vengeance". It features Black Frost proudly holding an advertisement for the game. I used this render to test out shadows and spotlights in Blender.

Changeling VR Work

From January 2024 to May 2024, I was brought on as a technical artist for Changeling VR. I was in charge of creating new assets or replacing old/borrowed assets. Here are my contributions.

Greenhouse

It includes the tree, wood platform, stairs, greenhouse, and picnic table. The greenhouse was the first assignment I had during Changeling VR.

Fingerpaint Scene

The fingerpaint scene includes multiple sheets of paper with artwork, as well as containers of paint. The drawings were created by me, and are meant to resemble one made by a child.

Fridge

The fridge is fully modeled on the inside and includes a wide variety of items. The items use simple textures as to not distract the player from the item they are supposed to collect in the fridge.

Hotdog Animation

I modeled a hotdog and had the construction worker sitting down to eat it indefinitely. Both the worker and bench were not modeled by me, only animated.

Case File

Originally a texture, the case file opens and closes to show level transitions. The drawing of the child was created by me.

Toddler Peeking Animation

The toddler was animated to be peeking around a corner. The toddler was not created by me, only animated.

Funky Mungos Work

For the fall semester of 2023 (August to December), I was in a class called IGM Production Studio, where the students were tasked to create a game by the end of the semester in a group. My group, consisting of seven people, created Funky Mungos, where I was the technical artist, creating all the models, textures, and animations. This was my first time as a technical artist, and I was teaching myself how to animate as I went along.

Player

The player is the main character of the game, their animations include being idle, running, jumping, falling, and swinging a weapon. In game, the weapon snaps to their left hand.

Mungo

The Mungo is the main collectible, as you are tasked with catching all the Mungos in a stage to win. The Mungo's animations include being idle and running.

Dinosaur

The dinosaur was a last minute edition to the game, as we realized that we didn't have a non-Mungo enemy. The animations include being idle, running, and attacking.

Weapons

The two weapons the player can use are a net and hammer. They snap to the player's left arm and can be switched with the press of a button.

Kingdom Key

This is the Keyblade "Kingdom Key" from the Kingdom Hearts series. I used this render to test out reflective materials and chain physics. Fun fact, the Kingdom Key is the first object I modeled on my own.

Jack Frost and Black Frost

This render features the characters Jack Frost and Black Frost from the Shin Megami Tensei Series. The characters are in the same poses from their official artwork. This is one of the first renders I made in Blender.

RPG Weezer

This is a render parodying the Weezer Blue Album cover. From left to right, the characters are Slime (Dragon Quest), Jack Frost (Shin Megami Tensei), Moogle (Final Fantasy), and Pikachu (Pokémon).

Bit the Computer

This is a model and render I made for a friend of mine, who created the character Bit. For this render, I learned how to add glow effects with Emission. I also learned about using 3D text in Blender.
