Here is a showcase of many games that I have created. The games were created in a wide variety of programs, including Unity and Unreal Engine. I will add my role in the development of the game. If the game is available to download/play, I will provide a link.

Apple of my Eye

In late October 2025, I participated in the Cozy Fall Jam 2025, which lasted four days. The theme was "lost and found", as well as a general fall theme. The game is a simple sidescroller where you must catch apples that fall from the trees, and deliver them to the drop off areas. I was the sole developer of this game, working on game/level design, programming, and art. The game was made in Unity.

Glued to the TV

In mid October 2025, I took part in the GameDev.tv Halloween Jam 2025, which lasted ten days. It has a visual theme featuring an old TV. The game is a 2D platformer with a CRT effect where you can buy checkpoints that get increasingly more expensive. I was the sole developer of this game, working on game/level design, programming, and art. The game was made in Unity.

Dokibird Reacts to Chains
